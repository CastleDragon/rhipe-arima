Rhipe ARIMA

This implementation of the ARIMA (Auto Regressive Integrated Moving Average) algorithm is based on R, Hadoop, and the RHIPE (Hree-pay) framework.

Why Rhipe Arima?

The ARIMA algorithm is used to understand time series data. Rhipe ARIMA parallelizes the algorithm over your entire cluster, allowing you to analyze larger datasets.
